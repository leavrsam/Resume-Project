Steps To Be a Better Mentor


  1. Willing to spend time
    • You have to be personally invested
    • If you want to impact others, you will have to spend a lot of time
    • Time is ideally spent face to face
  2. Have a plan
    • Mentoring will not happen by accident
    • Base the plan of of the mentees goals
    • Work together to create steps towards their goal
  3. Be encouraging
    • The first things out of your mouth should be upraising
    • Praise choices and behavior over natural talent
    • The actions that you encourage will be repeated
  4. Give needed correction and instruction
    • Be direct, bold and clear
    • Correct with kindness and love (show it with intonationand body language)
    • Explain your reasoning and share personal experience if you can
